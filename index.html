What is BACKERS & HACKERS?

Collabortion between Computer Science, Business students and the rest of the entrepreneurial community in Columbia. Local entrepreneurs come to our pitch event at the beginning of the Spring semester to present their ideas for mobile apps. Computer Science students pick the ideas they like and work on them for 3 months meanwhile Business students do market research and customer validation.

REASONS TO PITCH US YOUR IDEA

Completely free

Everything we are doing is completely free for people who come up with amazing ideas. Just make sure you can meet students on a regular basis to check their work, because nobody knows your future app better than you!

Mobile app and market research

If you are lucky and students like your idea, they will work hard to develop a mobile application for you. Business student may also pick your app to look at how viable from the business perspective.

Business workshops

A lot of people like what we do, especially serial entrepreneurs. They agreed to host business workshops to teach you how to turn Backers and Hackers into a successful business.

Publicity

At the end of the semester we are going to host a demo day - event where you can showcase your app. It will be a perfect opportunity for you to promote your business - we will invite people from local TV channels, as well as lots of journalists and photographers.
